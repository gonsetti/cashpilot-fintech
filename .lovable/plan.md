

# CashPilot — Fintech B2B Premium Landing Page

## Overview
Recreate the existing "Lefincer" landing page in React, applying a full strategic rebrand to **CashPilot** with a premium fintech visual identity. The structure, layout, animations, and functionality will be preserved exactly — only brand identity and color palette change.

## Brand Identity
- **Name:** CashPilot
- **Tagline:** "The Financial Control Center for Modern Companies"
- **CTAs:** "Start Free Trial", "Get Full Visibility", "Launch Dashboard"
- **Font:** Inter (Google Fonts) — 600 titles, 400-500 body, 700 KPIs

## Color Palette (replacing all pink/rose tones)
- **Primary background:** #0B1220 (deep navy)
- **Secondary background:** #0F172A
- **Cards:** #111827
- **Borders:** rgba(255,255,255,0.05)
- **Text primary:** #F9FAFB | **Text secondary:** #9CA3AF
- **Accent (buttons/highlights):** #22C55E (green) | **Hover:** #16A34A
- **Negative indicators:** #EF4444
- No pink gradients, neon glows, or colorized shadows
